About Me

I am an artist, copywriter,
and web designer from Watsonville, California living in Austin, Texas.

🏄 ---> 🌵

I am inspired by Modern art, comic books, graphic design, and video games.

I got my introduction to HTML through blogging for personal hobbies as well as work. I quickly realized I wanted to create an entire aesthetic to present my writing on, and the readily available free templates were either too complicated, or too boring, and I had no recourse as to how to change them. And thus began my adventures in web design.
