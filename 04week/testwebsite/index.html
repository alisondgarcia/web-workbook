Local Woman Finds Success in Web Development

Alison Dorantes-Garcia, a 28 year-old woman from Watsonville, CA, has finally broken out of her slump and found success in web development.


Alison went to college in the San Francisco Bay Area, initially intent on becomming a lawyer. Then that changed to teaching. And teaching was interesting, but not a passion. Alison found more pleasure in taking things apart and fixing them than she did in trying to negotiate problems in a giant, bureaucracy such as *Fake Name* Unified School District. She decided it was time to move onto something that was equally challenging and rewarding, and that something happened to be web development.


Alison took a class at Austin Coding Academy and decided to continue her learning trajectory, finishing the intermediate class, and eventually the advanced class. She is currently a video game developer for Rooster Teeth in Austin, Texas. she lives with her 2 blind pugs.
