LaCroix presents flavors based on the wildly popular manga and anime Jojo's Bizarre Adventure: Part 4. The central plot is about a local teen named Josuke Higashikata and his crew of friends. The flavors are based on the color palette and personality of each character.

Jotaro

Josuke wears mostly white and gold colors. This flavor is coconut and caramel.

Koichi

Koichi dresses in a traditional blue school uniform. He has a neurotic personality so his flavor is watermellon lime.

Rohan

Rohan dresses in shades of green. His flavor is lime.
