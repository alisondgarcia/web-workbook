About Me

I came to Austin, Texas from Watsonville, California, in search of adventure, freedom, and all of the other stuff they sing about in the Firefly intro.

Most of my direct work experience is in teaching and working with youth; I worked in Oakland Unified School District, Orinda, and Berkeley California schools with students from all ages and backgrounds. Teaching was challenging and interesting, although I dreamed of doing creative work, and started to transition to that through copywriting and editing.

I excell in team environments where I can fasciliatate direct communication. I'm blunt, and maybe too serious in spite of my hair color.

Why I am Learning how to Code

I have a fierce sense of aesthetic beauty that I've honed on personal artistic projects such as painting and digital illustration. I hope to apply my sense of color, balance, and appreciation of art to front-end engineering projects with an emphasis in user design.

Someone needs to make open-source projects beautiful, might as well be me.

I also hope to make video games.
